WHO WE ARE

The Netherlands-Myanmar Solidarity Platform Foundation is an ANBI-certified nonprofit dedicated to fostering solidarity between the Netherlands and Myanmar. Founded on principles of justice and freedom, we mobilize resources and support for Myanmar’s people during crises and beyond.

Our Commitment:

From emergency disaster relief to long-term advocacy, we stand with Myanmar’s communities to rebuild lives, restore dignity, and advance their revolutionary journey toward freedom and justice.

Our Mission & Vision:

Mission

To provide immediate humanitarian aid during crises while empowering Myanmar’s people through sustained advocacy, resource mobilization, and global solidarity.

Vision

A free, just, and resilient Myanmar where communities thrive without fear of oppression or disaster.

Why We Do It

Every meal, medical kit, and shelter delivered today plants a seed for a tomorrow where Myanmar’s people can pursue their aspirations unshackled by crisis or injustice.

Our Activities:

  • Current Focus: Earthquake Emergency Response
  • Distributing food, clean water, and temporary shelters.
  • Supplying rescue teams with equipment to save lives trapped under rubble.
  • Delivering urgent medical aid to prevent disease outbreaks.

Long-Term Initiatives:

  • Advocating for human rights and democratic freedoms in Myanmar.
  • Rebuilding schools and infrastructure to restore hope.
  • Partnering with global networks to amplify Myanmar’s voice.
